# Remembering Professor Tony

This repository holds a public memorial from Joe Hu for Professor Tony Bailetti.

Live memorial:
https://tony.hubeiqiao.com/

Source repository:
https://github.com/hubeiqiao/remembering-professor-tony

## Why This Exists

Professor Tony changed the way I saw myself, my work, and what I could become. I wanted one place that could remember him with honesty, warmth, and gratitude.

This memorial is not meant to be a formal biography. It is a personal tribute written from experience, with photos and moments that show how he encouraged me and many others.

## What This Memorial Holds

- A personal story about how Professor Tony shaped my path
- Photos that carry his warmth, presence, and spirit
- Public-facing words of thanks and remembrance
- A place where friends, students, and community members can understand why he mattered

## What I Hope People Feel Here

I hope this memorial feels respectful, human, and clear.

I hope people who knew Professor Tony can recognize something true in it.

I hope people who never met him can still understand his generosity, seriousness, and belief in other people.

## A Note From Joe

Professor Tony helped me take myself seriously. He gave weight to my ideas before they were obvious to other people. This memorial is one small way to say thank you.
