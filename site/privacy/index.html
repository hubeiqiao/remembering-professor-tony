Privacy

Privacy Policy

This memorial is a public static site. It includes Google Analytics to understand general traffic and site usage. There are no public forms or user accounts on the memorial itself.

Analytics

Google Analytics is present on the main memorial page to measure aggregate traffic and engagement.

Public pages

The site consists of public HTML pages and static assets meant to be readable by people, search engines, and AI crawlers.

Third-party hosting

The memorial is hosted with static delivery infrastructure that may generate standard access logs.

Questions

If you need to reach the author, use the memorial contact page.

Additional detail

This site uses Google Analytics for aggregate measurement rather than personal account management. The public pages are designed to be indexable and readable, which means automated agents may fetch them just as human visitors do. That visibility is intentional for this project.

The memorial does not ask visitors to submit forms, create accounts, or upload content. The site's main privacy posture is therefore simple: limited analytics, public HTML pages, and standard delivery logs from hosting infrastructure.

If you want to understand how Google Analytics works at a broader product level, you can consult Google Analytics documentation. If you need the author-facing contact route, use the contact page.

Because the site is intentionally open to search crawlers and AI bots, some automated access is expected. That policy is part of the purpose of the site: it is meant to be discoverable, readable, and citeable rather than hidden behind friction or authentication.

The privacy tradeoff is therefore narrow and visible. The site exposes public pages and uses limited analytics to understand traffic patterns, but it does not collect the kind of rich user account data that would require a more complex privacy program.

Review the sources page to understand what public materials are intentionally exposed.
