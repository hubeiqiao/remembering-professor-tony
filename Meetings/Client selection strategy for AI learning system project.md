# Client selection strategy for AI learning system project

Fri, 19 Sept 25

### Project Overview - AI Learning System

- Building learning framework to bridge “knowing-doing gap” in education
- Current AI gives direct answers but lacks observational capability for learning effectiveness
- Initial focus: English speaking practice for ESL learners
- Personal example: 1 year practice with previous product showed no quantified improvement in speaking
- Research methodology:
  - Filtered from 2000 to 100 to 40 relevant papers
  - Three research objectives:
    - Behavior sensing for scale accuratization
    - Integration mechanism bridging knowing-doing gap
    - AI applications in ESL speaking practice

### Client Selection Challenge

- Current target: AGI Ventures (Hy and Nilda)
- Advisor’s assessment: Poor client fit
  - No business focus on education or ESL
  - Business model around hackathons/AI events, not education
  - Cannot provide valuable resources or data access
  - Limited value exchange potential

### Alternative Client Recommendations

- Immigrant support organizations in Ottawa (10+ available)
  - Direct access to ESL learners needing language skills
  - Real user data for research
  - Clear mutual value proposition
- Educational institutions with similar AI tutoring needs
  - Example: Entrepreneurship education using AI tutors
  - Scalable teaching methodology applications
  - Broader principle applicability beyond ESL

### Value Proposition Analysis

- Current approach lacks strategic client selection
- Need clients who provide:
  - Access to target users/data
  - Domain expertise and feedback
  - Resources for project advancement
- Avoid “paper client” relationships with minimal mutual benefit
- Focus on rich value exchange over reputation points

### Current Status

- Has existing users for product testing
- Original backup plan: self as client
- Access to user data currently limited
- Considering advisor feedback on client strategy refinement
