# AI tools infrastructure and research-to-business translation strategy

Fri, 08 Aug 25

### Strategic Framework Development

- Transitioning from competence-facing to customer-facing approach
- Three-node framework triangle for AI tools infrastructure:
  - Core AI tools (ChatGPT-5 as primary platform)
  - Supplements (industry-specific tools for particular business needs)
  - Infrastructure support systems
- Focus shift: from “making the sausage” to “selling the sausage”
  - Customers don’t care about internal processes
  - Must view everything from customer perspective

### Research-to-Business Translation Model

- New CMO (Context-Mechanism-Outcome) logic framework implementation
  - Context: problem definition and environment
  - Mechanism: internal company adjustments needed
  - Outcome: measurable business results
- Three-stage process development:
  - Foundation stage: research outputs + problem identification
  - Solution design: connect research to business problems
  - Refinement: customer-driven improvements

### Stage 1: Foundation Requirements

- Problem definition template needed for clients:
  - Current state vs desired future state
  - Obstacles to overcome
  - Timeline and priorities
- Context elements to capture:
  - Company type, industry trends
  - Regulatory landscape
  - Strategic priorities
  - Stakeholders and constraints
  - Scope of analysis
- Research outputs classification (7 types identified)
  - Frameworks, theories, and models most critical

### Stage 2: Solution Development

- Connect research findings to specific business problems
- Proposed solution design using AI tools
- Stakeholder validation process (“resonance testing”)
- Components to include:
  - Benefits and potential risks
  - Cause-and-effect relationships
  - Implementation considerations
  - Competitive solution analysis

### Student Career Success Insights

- Three distinct graduate outcome tiers observed:
  - Top tier: $100K+ offers (first time in 40+ years)
  - Middle tier: $70K-90K operational roles
  - Bottom tier: $40-45K or unemployed
- Three critical success differentiators:
  - AI project evidence (outcomes first, then AI tools used)
  - Start-to-finish project completion capability
  - Value proposition communication skills
- Talent-first approach: build skills before defining products
- Need to eliminate underperforming students from program

### ChatGPT-5 Implementation Strategy

- Focus on distinctive capabilities vs ChatGPT-4
- Apply new features to solve real business problems
- Avoid generic AI teaching - emphasize problem-solving applications
- Students must demonstrate AI project evidence in portfolios
- Framework thinking essential to manage increased AI capability

### Next Steps and Refinements

- Template development for customer problem intake
- CMO framework iteration based on feedback
- Stage definitions refinement before implementation
- Presentation gates between each stage
- Focus on iterative, living document approach
