"Up Our Game, Canada!" Checkpoint Meeting with Sprott School of Business 

May 12, 2025

This meeting showcased the innovative work of several student teams developing AI-enhanced tools to support Canadian SMEs. Professor Howard Nemiroff (Dean), Prof Nusa Fain, and Prof Eduardo Bailetti attended to learn about the teams' work.

### Core Products

- **Advantage Builder (Joe)**: An AI platform that generates comprehensive business documents for SMEs using Gemini 2.5. Demonstrated with "Capital Organic," creating a market entry strategy document in minutes that includes executive summaries, market analysis, go-to-market strategies, and financial outlooks.
- **Scoping Review Guide (Rachel)**: A structured methodology for conducting evidence-based research to support SME decision-making. Available in multiple formats (PDF, interactive web version, videos), it follows a 9-step process that combines AI efficiency with human oversight.

### Core Competencies

- **AI Chatbot (Ravindra)**: A specialized academic assistant that supports students conducting scoping reviews, offering greater precision than general AI tools. Cloud-hosted and continuously learning from user interactions while maintaining privacy.
- **Media Production (Jia)**: Demonstrated capabilities in creating professional videos and podcasts that make complex topics accessible. Uses tools like Gamma for design and leverages AI for script development.
- **Human Oversight (Ellen)**: Framework ensuring AI outputs are accurate and reliable through three pillars: control, understanding, and education. Implements principles from the AI Act with templates for documentation and error tracking.
- **Deep Research (Joe)**: Guide for using AI to synthesize hundreds of information sources in 5-30 minutes rather than weeks. Compared different research tools (ChatGPT, Perplexity Pro, Gemini) and provides a six-step methodology.
- **Process Efficiency (Richard & Song)**: Created tools to streamline literature reviews, focusing on often-overlooked grey literature. Developed an AI-powered assistant that integrates with 457+ databases and provides step-by-step automation.

### Educational Impact Discussion

The meeting concluded with a discussion about how these technologies transform education. The group addressed the pushback from Arts and Social Sciences departments concerned about academic integrity, comparing AI adoption to historical technology transitions like cameras in art.
